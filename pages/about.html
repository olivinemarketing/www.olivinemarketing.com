Who We Are
We are not your typical marketing agency. Our niche is working with fast-growing, developer-focused tech companies -- and actually understanding what they do. Continuous Integration? Containers? Selenium? Yes, we know what that means. Learn More

Ashley Wilson

Ashley Wilson is the creator of Olivine Marketing. With 8 years of marketing experience in the SaaS / technology space, she loves taking complex technical products and building a story that engages developers and enterprises alike.

Prior to Olivine, Ashley was an early employee at Sauce Labs, where she held roles in marketing, sales and biz dev. During her time as Head of Marketing, she created a well-loved brand in the software development space and organized dozens of developer-focused meetups and conferences. Ashley is also a singer-songwriter and avid traveler.

Paula Carvalho

Paula, who handles creative direction and design for Olivine, is a Brazilian design consultant specializing in Art Direction, UI/UX, Visual Design and Branding with 10+ years of experience with a strong background in graphic design. Her approach is creating visual concepts that integrate brand and experience to drive user engagement.

Paula’s passions are fashion, photography, technology and she loves to leave all that to escape to nature.

Todd Wilson

Todd Wilson is Olivine’s Executive Producer and the owner of Fraser Film Group, the full-service production company that handles all our videos. Todd’s 25+ years of experience as an award-winning producer, director and cameraman is broad and deep, and his unique approach to storytelling and filmmaking has served his clients well.

As the operating partner at Creative Digital Group in Atlanta, acquired by LBi in 2008, the company flourished and was recognized as a powerhouse in marketing.Since then, Todd has directed, filmed and produced work for a wide array of clients, including Home Depot, Simmons Bedding, Graco, GlaxoSmithKline, and Sauce Labs. He is one of the co-producers of Black Road, a feature film released in the fall of 2015.

Raechel Lambert

Raechel, who helps with product marketing and handles product launches for Olivine, drove some of Intercom's largest product releases and has worked for technical products in the developer tools space. Formerly she worked in finance and brings her analytical mindset and focus on business goals to her work in marketing.
